ABOUT TARANATH GROUP

Where Expertise Meets Ambition

CS. Taranath Bingimalla

Founder & CEO — Taranath Group

CS. Taranath Bingimalla is an ICSI-qualified Company Secretary, Chartered Financial Analyst, and seasoned EXIM Consultant with over two decades of institutional experience. He has held senior roles across ICICI Bank, HSBC, and Aditya Birla Financial Services — building expertise at the intersection of capital markets, regulatory compliance, and international trade. In 2020, he channelled this institutional knowledge into founding Taranath Group — a multi-vertical enterprise operating across financial advisory, India-China trade, and luxury interiors. The Group is built on a single conviction: that the same rigour that governs institutional finance can — and should — be applied to entrepreneurial growth.

Co-led by Shravani R — MBA graduate from PESIT College, Bangalore, with professional experience at Deloitte.
